JD'S 7 Round NYG's Mock Draft



The NFL playoffs coming to an end can only mean one thing, Draft Season is upon us. The New York Giants have been nothing
short of awful the past few years. This off-season, fans were calling for the firing of General Manager Dave Gettleman and Head Coach Pat Shurmur,
but Gettleman's job was spared. This year will be a make or break for Gettleman who was brought here to rebuild the team, but after
many questionable draft picks and off-season moves, things are not looking good.
